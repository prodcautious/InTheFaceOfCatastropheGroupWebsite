Praise

"Terrifying and uplifting by turns, In the Face of Catastrophe is a memoir
about the everyday suffering and silver linings discovered while working
through a health crisis with family members to assist. This idea of presence
as a gift is the book’s most tangible and inspiring quality." ~ Foreword Clarion

"In the Face of Catastrophe alternates between understandable despair and hopeful
bromides, lessons, and insights from the experience, especially on embracing the
power of presence in the everyday. Goldman’s journey toward recovery is heartening,
and the family’s accounts will offer comfort to others facing similar trials." ~ Publishers weekly booklife

"Goldman and Hirschleifer deserve plaudits for this one-of-a-kind offering that will
be a powerful inspiration to those who struggle with health crises without assistance." ~ Readers favorite

"A beautiful, exhilarating account of gratitude and resilience, and how one family
faced catastrophe and found unexpected blessings. A story of stoicism through unimaginable
crisis. Anyone wondering how they’ll overcome even the darkest challenge will find light in
these fast-turning pages." ~ Steve Israel

"It’s easy to lose hope when a friend or loved one struggles with a serious medical condition
or becomes critically ill. Jennifer Goldman and Caryn Hirshleifer, in their book, In the Face
of Catastrophe, tell us how they remained positive when faced with a devastating loss. Their
heartwarming story encourages us all to keep fighting to overcome the odds and provides hope
to all who need it. Miracles can happen. And they do. I recommend this book to my constituents
and friends who are facing difficult times." ~ Paul Feiner

"This is an inspiring story of recovery and renewal against the odds. It teaches us how the power
of family and community can carry us through life’s toughest times and greatest challenges." ~ LL Cool Jay
