About the Authors

Caryn Meg Hirshleifer & Jennifer Rose Goldman

Caryn Meg Hirshleifer

Caryn Meg Hirshleifer is a co-owner of Hirshleifers, a highly successful fifth-generation luxury retail business located in Manhasset, New York, where she serves as general counsel as well as in the self-described roles of store historian, store philosopher, and staff nurturer. Before that, she worked as an attorney in New York City government, including her time as general counsel to the New York City Fire Department and, before that, as general counsel to Mayor Koch’s Office of Homelessness. When not at work, Caryn is reading, writing, or practicing riffs on her electric Fender Mustang bass guitar.

Caryn also sits on the Board of the Long Island Council on Alcoholism and Drug Dependence.

Jennifer Rose Goldman

Before her stroke, Jennifer Rose Goldman, a 2013 graduate of Skidmore College with a joint degree in religion and philosophy, founded and served as CEO of Essential Rose, an aromatherapy beauty brand that she started in her college dormitory room. Essential Rose packaged and sold teas and skin-care products for inner, rather than outer, beauty. A motivational speaker on wellness and self-care, she created her own content and taught a six-week course called “Lead Your Life.” Jenn is passionate about all things spiritual and loves discovering any new modalities she can immerse herself in.


Illustrations by Amanda Gwen Goldman

Amanda Gwen Goldman

Amanda Gwen Goldman, younger sister of Jenn and daughter of Caryn, has a passion for mixing colors and integrating textural elements into her drawings. The book illustrations are complex and gritty and speak to the dialectic between the beauty and lightness of her color presentation as contrasted with the rugged texture reflecting the heaviness under which the illustrations were conceived.

Amanda also loves the artistry of fashion and is dedicated to finding justice and would ultimately like to help falsely incarcerated prisoners.
